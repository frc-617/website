Our Work

Our organization is primarily student-led. Hype team works to raise money for the team through planning events, fundraising at school activities, contacting potential sponsors, and writing grants. The mechanical team builds the robot, and our electrical team is responsible for correctly wiring the robot. Programming, or coding team, builds the team website and programs the robot to ensure that it does what is required of it. These sub categories of team 617 are all run by students.
